How to read a phylogenetic tree

  • Each bubble represents a cetacean species.
  • Hover and click on a species to learn more about them!
  • A node between two species represents a genetic divergence between them.
  • The longer the line between two species, the more genetically different they are.
  • Find the red node: this is the Crown Cetacea, the oldest common ancestor of all cetaceans.
  • Biologically similar species may belong to the same Genus (small group of species) or to the same Family (bigger group of species).
  • Images with the same color outline belong to the same family.
